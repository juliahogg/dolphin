About

Welcome to Flipper Finder! It’s a fin-tastic day to learn about dolphins! At Flipper Finder, we use accurate, reliably sourced data to educate the public about how dolphins eat, move, and survive. We also have some fun minigames where you can learn the anatomy of a dolphin or where the 40 different species of dolphins live around the world! Sit back and relax, we promise you’ll dolphinately have a great time!
